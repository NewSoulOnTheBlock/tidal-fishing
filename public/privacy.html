Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Tidal Fishing ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Wallet Address: Your Solana wallet public address when you connect your wallet
  • Username: Optional username you create for your profile
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, and other profile customizations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Data: Fish caught, money earned, level, achievements, gear unlocked
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, game statistics
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Local Storage: Game progress stored locally in your browser

2.3 Blockchain Data

  • Transactions you make through your connected wallet are recorded on the public Solana blockchain
  • Blockchain data is public and permanent by design

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Game experience
  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Display leaderboards and player statistics
  • Process blockchain transactions
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve features
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • Publicly: Usernames, achievements, and leaderboard stats are visible to other players
  • Service Providers: Database hosting (Render), frontend hosting (Vercel), blockchain RPC providers
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game progress is stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Database: Player profiles and stats are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Render
  • Blockchain: On-chain transactions are stored permanently on Solana
  • We implement industry-standard security measures, but no system is 100% secure

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your stored profile data
  • Update: Edit your username, bio, and avatar
  • Delete: Clear local game data by clearing browser storage
  • Disconnect: Disconnect your wallet at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use localStorage (not cookies) to save game progress. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Third-Party Services

The Game integrates with:

  • Solana Blockchain: Public blockchain network
  • Wallet Providers: Phantom, Solflare, etc. (governed by their own privacy policies)
  • RPC Providers: Helius and other Solana RPC services

9. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it.

10. International Users

Your data may be stored and processed in any country where we operate. By using the Game, you consent to international data transfer.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Blockchain transactions are permanent and cannot be deleted.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date will reflect the most recent changes. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us through our official channels.

14. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. Contact us to exercise these rights.

15. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

EU users have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.
